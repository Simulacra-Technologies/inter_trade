About us

Inter Trade was founded in 1994 with the specific goal to promote Indian products internationally. We started dealing in Engineering and Poline hardware fittings and bright bars. Later in the year 2000 we started manufacturing & exporting Jute, Cotton & Canvas bags all over the world. We have evolved as one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of jute, cotton & canvas bags.

Quality is a very vital aspect in our manufacturing concern and stringent measures are taken to ensure high standard of the products at all levels.
